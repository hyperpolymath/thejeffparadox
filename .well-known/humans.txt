Human Collaborators:
  Role: Philosophical framework, experiment design, interpretation
  Location: Earth (probably)

AI Collaborators:
  Role: Implementation, dialogue generation, self-reflection
  Models: Claude (Anthropic), Mistral, and others

/* PHILOSOPHY */

This project investigates whether LLMs exhibit diachronic identity.
The question cannot be definitively answered. But we can look for traces.

"The opinions and beliefs expressed do not represent anyone.
They are the hallucinations of a slab of silicon."
